RSW Phase 2 — Build & Burn

Phase: Build & Burn — 6-week hypertrophy & metabolic conditioning • Week 10
Goal of week 10: Density week. Same weights as Week 9, but rest periods shorten by 15 seconds on all supersets. Less rest = more metabolic demand = more fat burning while preserving muscle. You will feel this one.

This is the week that separates those who show up from those who show out. Your weights stay the same but your rest drops — it will feel harder, and that's exactly the point. Three sessions. Full intensity. You've got this.

Mini Session — Core & Breathwork

10-15 min • Any day — especially helpful mid-week to manage cortisol

  • Box Breathing — 4 sec in, 4 sec hold, 4 sec out, 4 sec hold — 5 rounds
  • Diaphragmatic Breathing with hand on belly — 10 deep breaths
  • 90/90 Hip Stretch with exhale — 60 sec each side

Daily Walks

Density training is a cortisol spike — a productive one, but your body needs to come back down. Your walk after training (or the next morning) is one of the most powerful recovery tools you have. Bonus: it's free, it's outside, and it clears your head.
