RSW Phase 2 — Build & Burn

Phase: Build & Burn — 6-week hypertrophy & metabolic conditioning • Week 9
Goal of week 9: Load week. This week you increase weight on every compound lift by 5–10 lbs while dropping reps slightly to 10-12. You're shifting from volume adaptation to load adaptation. This is where strength and muscle are built simultaneously.

You've earned this week. Two weeks of building volume means your body is ready to handle more load. Pick up something heavier than you think you can — then do it with perfect form. That's the whole game.

Progression cue: This should feel significantly heavier than Week 7. Add 10–15 lbs from your Week 7 weight.

Leg Press (feet high and wide) 4 sets • 12 reps • 75 sec rest • Tempo: 3-0-1
Progression cue: Add one plate per side from your highest week 8 weight.
Pattern: squat|glute
Banded Glute Kickback (cable or band) 3 sets • 12 each leg reps • 45 sec rest • Tempo: 1-2-1
Progression cue: Add a 2-second squeeze at peak contraction. Increase resistance if available.

Plank Hold (full) 3 sets • 30-45 sec hold reps • 45 sec rest • Tempo: isometric
Progression cue: Brace like you're about to take a punch. Progress to 60 sec when 45 sec feels manageable.

Progression cue: Add 10 lbs from Week 7. Reps drop from 12 to 10 — load increases.

Pattern: row
SUPERSETSUPERSET B2: Dumbbell Lateral Raise 3 sets • 12 reps • 60 sec after B2 rest • Tempo: 2-0-2
Progression cue: Increase by 2.5 lbs. Shoulders stay down, elbows soft.

Face Pull (cable, rope attachment) 3 sets • 15 reps • 45 sec rest • Tempo: 2-1-2
Progression cue: Slight weight increase only if external rotation is maintained through all reps.

SUPERSETSUPERSET C1: Dumbbell Bicep Curl 3 sets • 10 reps • 0 sec (go to C2) rest • Tempo: 2-0-2
Progression cue: Heavier than Weeks 7-8. Control the negative — don't drop the weight.

SUPERSETSUPERSET C2: Tricep Rope Pushdown 3 sets • 12 reps • 60 sec after C2 rest • Tempo: 2-0-2
