RSW Phase 2 — Build & Burn

Phase: Build & Burn — 6-week hypertrophy & metabolic conditioning • Week 7
Goal of week 7: Establish your new baseline. Focus on feeling the muscle — slow down your tempo, especially on the way down. Every rep should be intentional. This week is about building the mind-muscle connection that will drive your results over the next 6 weeks.

Welcome to Phase 2 — this is where the real transformation begins. You've built your foundation over the last 6 weeks, and now it's time to put that strength to work. Three focused sessions a week is all you need when you train with intention and purpose.

Monday: Lower A – Glute & Quad Focus

Your progress is saved on this device

Barbell Hip Thrust 3 sets • 12-15 reps • 60 sec rest • Tempo: 2-0-1
Progression cue: Add 5 lbs when you can complete all 3 sets at 15 reps with full hip extension and a 1-second squeeze at the top.
Pattern: glute
Goblet Squat (heels elevated on plate) 3 sets • 12-15 reps • 60 sec rest • Tempo: 3-1-1
Progression cue: Increase dumbbell weight by 5 lbs when you can hit 15 reps with a full 3-second descent and upright torso.
Pattern: squat
Reverse Lunge (dumbbell) 3 sets • 10 each leg reps • 60 sec rest • Tempo: 2-0-1
Progression cue: Increase weight by 5 lbs or switch to a barbell when form is solid for all reps.
Pattern: squat|glute
Leg Press (feet high and wide) 3 sets • 15 reps • 60 sec rest • Tempo: 3-0-1
Progression cue: Add one plate per side when all 15 reps feel controlled through the full range of motion.
Pattern: squat|glute
Banded Glute Kickback (cable or band) 3 sets • 15 each leg reps • 45 sec rest • Tempo: 1-1-1
Progression cue: Increase cable weight by 2.5 lbs or move to a heavier band when you can complete all reps with full hip extension and no hip rotation.
Pattern: glute
Plank with Alternating Hip Tap 3 sets • 10 each side reps • 45 sec rest • Tempo: controlled
Progression cue: Progress to a plank with alternating leg lift when hip taps feel stable.
Pattern: core

SUPERSETSUPERSET A1: Lat Pulldown (wide grip) 3 sets • 12 reps • 0 sec (go to A2) rest • Tempo: 2-1-2
Progression cue: Increase weight by 5 lbs when you can complete 12 reps with full lat engagement and no momentum.
Pattern: pull
SUPERSETSUPERSET A2: Dumbbell Chest Press (incline) 3 sets • 12 reps • 60 sec after A2 rest • Tempo: 3-0-1
Progression cue: Increase dumbbells by 5 lbs when all reps are controlled with full range of motion.
Pattern: push
SUPERSETSUPERSET B1: Seated Cable Row (close grip) 3 sets • 12 reps • 0 sec (go to B2) rest • Tempo: 2-1-2
Progression cue: Add weight when you can complete 12 reps keeping shoulders down and chest tall throughout.
Pattern: row
SUPERSETSUPERSET B2: Dumbbell Lateral Raise 3 sets • 12-15 reps • 60 sec after B2 rest • Tempo: 2-0-2
Progression cue: Increase weight by 2.5 lbs when you can hit 15 reps without shrugging shoulders.
Pattern: push
Face Pull (cable, rope attachment) 3 sets • 15 reps • 45 sec rest • Tempo: 2-1-2
Progression cue: Increase weight when you can complete all reps with external rotation at the end position. This is a postural priority — never sacrifice form for load.
Pattern: pull
SUPERSETSUPERSET C1: Dumbbell Bicep Curl 3 sets • 12 reps • 0 sec (go to C2) rest • Tempo: 2-0-2
Progression cue: Increase by 2.5 lbs when all 12 reps are controlled with no swinging.
Pattern: curl
SUPERSETSUPERSET C2: Tricep Rope Pushdown 3 sets • 12-15 reps • 60 sec after C2 rest • Tempo: 2-0-2

Pattern: push

Friday: Lower B – Glute & Posterior Chain

Your progress is saved on this device

Romanian Deadlift (barbell or dumbbells) 3 sets • 12 reps • 60 sec rest • Tempo: 3-1-1
Progression cue: Add 10 lbs (barbell) or 5 lbs (dumbbells) when you complete 12 reps with a full hip hinge and hamstring stretch at the bottom on every rep.
Pattern: hinge
Single-Leg RDL (dumbbell, pause at bottom) 3 sets • 10 each leg reps • 60 sec rest • Tempo: 3-2-1
Progression cue: Increase weight by 5 lbs when balance and control are solid for all reps with a 2-second pause.
Pattern: hinge|glute
Bulgarian Split Squat (rear foot elevated) 3 sets • 10 each leg reps • 60 sec rest • Tempo: 3-0-1
Progression cue: Add 5 lbs per dumbbell when you can complete all reps with a full 3-second descent and knee tracking over toes.
Pattern: squat|glute
Lying Hamstring Curl (machine) 3 sets • 12-15 reps • 60 sec rest • Tempo: 2-1-3
Progression cue: Increase weight when you hit 15 reps with a 3-second eccentric (lowering) on every rep. The slow lower is the money.
Pattern: hinge
Glute Bridge with Band (pulse + hold) 3 sets • 10 pulses + 10 sec hold reps • 45 sec rest • Tempo: pulse then isometric
Progression cue: Progress to single-leg version or add a plate across hips when you can complete all pulses and holds without losing tension in the band.
Pattern: glute
Dead Bug (slow and controlled) 3 sets • 8 each side reps • 45 sec rest • Tempo: 4-count lower, 4-count raise
Progression cue: Add a 1 kg ankle weight or dumbbell in extended hand when all reps are completed with lower back pressed into the floor.
Pattern: core

Mini Session — Core & Parasympathetic Recovery

10-15 min • Any day — morning, between sessions, or before bed

  • 90/90 Hip Stretch — 60 sec each side, deep breathing
  • Cat-Cow with Rib Cage Breath — 10 slow reps
  • Dead Bug — 5 each side, slow and intentional

Daily Walks

Your daily walk is non-negotiable — not as cardio, but as medicine. 20–30 minutes of walking stabilizes blood sugar, lowers cortisol, and supports progesterone levels. This is especially important in the days before your period. Put on a podcast, leave your phone in your pocket, and move.
